Iteration 5 - Design sprint - Completing tasks on mobile devices

Tested 25 February 2021

We know that Probation Practitioners save up their recording tasks until they can get back to their desks and input into Delius.

Enable Probation Practitioners to complete quick recording tasks during and between meetings with service users, reducing the administrative burden when they get back to their desk?

Tasks covered:

  • Scenario 1 - Mark an SU as complied
  • Scenario 2 - Arrange the next session
  • Scenario 3 - Update SUs personal details - Address and phone number
Outputs
  • Users welcomed the idea of being able to use their mobiles to record information directly into the system
  • Users would use it as a reference to check details on a case if they are making a home visit
  • Users are already using their mobiles - but as a workaround. They then have to add information into the system manually.
  • Users want to see the type of appointment on their calendar view, so they can prepare
  • Needed option for evening time slots, as they sometimes have late appointments
  • Users found ‘Other names’ confusing, and used the term ‘Aliases’
  • A dictation feature would help when adding case notes (user tested with dyslexia who uses Dragon)
  • Users wanted to be able to integrate with their phone contacts (eg: when updating mobile number)
